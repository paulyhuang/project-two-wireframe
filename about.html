The Chinese Students Association has been one of WUSTL's premier cultural groups since 1995.

CSA generally puts on events for the student body monthly to share and promote awareness for Chinese and Chinese-American cultural and societal issues while supporting a social environment for students of all backgrounds at WashU.

CSA is mainly composed of four components open to anyone at WashU:

  1. General Body Events - These events often include free Bubble Tea information sessions, Lantern Festival Celebration, Tang Yuan nights, Study Breaks with Chinese delicacies, etc. Within the first month of school, we also plan to host a "CSA Answers your Questions," where upperclassmen will share the inside scoop about the majors and classes at WashU.

  2. Speaker Events - Last year, we invited the Angry Asian Man to campus. In the past, we have also invited Harry Shum Jr. from Glee. This year, we hope to invite more outside speakers to campus and to host events with professors at WashU.

  3. Annual CSA Formal - Every November before Thanksgiving, CSA hosts a formal, a time for friends to come together and relax right before the start of finals crunch time. The venue is a secret and is not released to the public until the day of, so be sure to tune in and buy your tickets in early November!

  4. Community Service - CSA is also dedicated to fostering a family environment for the students at WashU, and one of the best ways to get to know people is to do community service together! Past community service opportunities include spending time with Chinese elders at a local senior center (good way to practice your Chinese!), being a part of the Green Team and seeing a free Cardinals game, volunteering at an animal shelter, translating medical terms for Chinese speakers, and many others.

CSA is comprised of a general body of undergraduates at WashU who are invited to all events put on by CSA. If you want to be added to the email list to get more updates about events, please contact us at csa@su.wustl.edu or through the primary contact.

CSA is also comprised of an executive board of around 15-20 students that coordinate and execute events for the student body. Elections are held semesterly - freshman representative positions in the fall and other vacancies in the spring semester. There are many opportunities to get involved and to be a part of the family, whether it is through going to our events, gaining a leadership role in one of our committees, or becoming a member of our executive board!

We are currently looking for potential executive members for the 2015-2016 academic school year. If you are passionate and dedicated and are looking for a way to expand your leadership skills, please consider applying to be an executive member of CSA! Reach out to us through the contact forms provided on this website, and we will set up a meeting time as soon as possible to discuss your potential to be a part of a great organization.
